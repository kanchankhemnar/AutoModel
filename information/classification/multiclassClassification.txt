# :dart: Multiclass Classification

Multiclass classification is used when the target has more than two categories.

Examples:
- Flower species: Iris-setosa, Iris-versicolor, Iris-virginica
- Digit recognition: 0 through 9

Popular algorithms:
- Decision Tree Classifier
- KNN
- Multinomial Naive Bayes
- Logistic Regression (with multiclass support)
